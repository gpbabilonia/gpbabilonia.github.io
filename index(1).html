About

I am a rural development professional and researcher with over a decade of experience working across agrifood systems, value chain development, and climate adaptation — primarily in the Philippines and Southeast Asia.

I hold a dual master's degree in Rural Development from Ghent University, Universidad de Córdoba, Università di Pisa, and Can Tho University, completed magna cum laude as a VLIR-UOS scholar. My dissertation on gendered food security in rice-shrimp farming systems under climate stress won the Future Proef Award 2025 at UGent.

I am equally at home writing academic papers, running multi-stakeholder coordination, and facilitating capacity-building workshops for farmers and MSMEs in the field.
